How to Play

  • Arrow Keys: Move your player
  • Spacebar: Shoot
  • Enter: Restart after defeat

Superbosses

  • The Teleporter: Warps across the screen, making it hard to predict. Tip: Time your shots when it reappears.
  • The Speedster: Extremely fast movement. Tip: Keep your distance and fire continuously.
  • The Twins: Two bosses at once with independent movement. Tip: Focus on one first to reduce pressure.
  • The Titan: Slower but is massive and can more easily corner you. Tip: Keep moving and chip away at its health.
  • The Shield-Bearer: Has a shield that rotates counter-clockwise, blocking shots and reflecting them back at you. Tip: Shoot from the exposed side when the shield rotates.
  • The Gunner: Shoots back at you. Tip: Use corners to dodge and peek to shoot.
