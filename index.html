Welcome to Boss Rush

Boss Rush is a fast-paced survival game where you take on an endless wave of bosses, each more powerful than the last. The objective is simple: survive, defeat, and repeat.

Game Highlights

  • Boss Variety: Face multiple unique superbosses, each with different behaviors and abilities.
  • Scaling Difficulty: Every boss becomes tougher with each respawn, increasing in both health and resistance.
  • Dynamic Combat: Learn to dodge, shoot, and counter enemy patterns to stay alive.
  • Smooth Controls: Use the arrow keys to move and spacebar to shoot — simple yet challenging.

Visual Interface

  • Health Bars: Both you and the boss have visible health bars. Stay alive and track your damage.
  • Score Panel: Keep track of how many bosses you’ve defeated in the top-left corner.

What Makes Boss Rush Different?

Unlike typical enemy spam games, Boss Rush is built around smart design — every boss is handcrafted with unique traits and escalating mechanics. Victory requires pattern recognition, sharp reflexes, and strategy.
